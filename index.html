Bournemouth was recently voted the top digital city outside of London

That’s right, that little seaside town you thought only your grandparents flocked to for a cream tea in their beach hut has been voted the top digital city outside of London. No longer are we a hub for OAPs, we are now a digital hub, rich with fresh talent and awesome agencies creating amazing things.

It’s not just the Bournemouth based agencies we have to thank for our ‘digital revival’ – we have a fantastic university considered the go to education centre for all things digital and creative.

The list is endless for opportunities in Bournemouth. It’s a great place to work, to live and to socialise…

Choose life, choose Bournemouth.

Reasons why Bournemouth is
AWESOME
1 Location

Seven miles of golden beaches lines our wonderful town – head a 10 minutes west and see some of the most incredible views in the UK. From Bournemouth Pier and the glamorous houses lining Sandbanks to Old Harry Rocks and Lulworth cove, we really do have one of the best coastlines around.

And don’t worry about missing out on the great nightlife and shopping – we have it all right here.

Cost of Living

Whether you are looking to buy a property, get a gym membership or even just buy a pint, you will find that prices in London are significantly higher than in Bournemouth.

The average salary in Bournemouth may be slightly lower than what is offered in London but you’ll find yourself being able to spend that disposable income on a beach hut, after work drinks, anything else that takes your fancy. There’s no worry of the cost of an annual tube pass, extortionate property rental rates and the general high prices of day-to-day living. Imagine that?
